Project Title : automatic water level detector system

the diagram above shows the prototype of the system

about the project

Problem Description

1. Wastage of water due to manual control or human negligence.

2. Frequent manual control, time wasting and users effort.

Solution Description

A prototype that monitors water level in the tank and pumping of water from source.

Reducing risks of water Wastage and conserving electrical energy.

Potential Impact

Reducing water wastage.

Ensure that water is always available for use.

Principle of Operation

When water level in the tank is at minimum level, sensor sends signal to the control unit

Then control unit processes signal and then tells relay switch to switch on and start pumping water from the source to fill in tank.

When water level in the tank is at maximum level, sensor sends signal to the control unit

Then control unit processes signal and then tells relay switch to switch off and stop pumping water from the source.

This process repeats itself depending on the level of water in the tank.

Future Design Plans

To add sensors at the source to as well monitor the level of water in the source.
